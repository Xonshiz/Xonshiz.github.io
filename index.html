Cab Management System {SalesForce}

This is a very basic Cab Management System made for SalesForce Platform as our Summer Training Project. We developed it within 1 week, while learning SalesForce side by side, so it's not going to be the BEST logic or cleanest work/project you've ever seen. This project was developed with the mindset of "Using SalesForce without letting the users know about it". What does that even mean? We wanted to create a website where users can book a cab, get the cab assigned and then get the bills on their registered E-Mail IDs.All this on SalesForce platform, but the user won't see anything related to SalesForce. They should simply see the website, do their work and be done with it. That's it.
